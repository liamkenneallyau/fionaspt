Fiona Kenneally Personal Training

Supporting Women's Health and Fitness in the Dubai Marina

Since 2022, I have transitioned away from my background in elite sport to help everyday women hit their goals.

Whether they are to look better, get stronger, or just be happier in their own skin.

With over 20 years of experience coaching female gymnasts at the highest level, I have worked with 5 Australian Olympians.

I spent 10 years as the Head Coach of the Victorian Institute of Sport Gymnastics Program and have done consulting work for elite gymnastics federations in Singapore and Australia.

Exercise is, and should always be, for everyone. My goal is always to create a supportive, kind environment, and provide practical advice that helps you tackle the challenges of mid-life with physical strength and mental joy.
